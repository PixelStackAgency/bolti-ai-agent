# Privacy Policy - Bolti AI

**Last Updated: November 28, 2025**

## 1. Information We Collect

- **Account Information**: Phone number, company name, email, industry type
- **Call Data**: Call recordings, transcripts, phone numbers, duration, timestamps
- **Knowledge Base**: PDFs, URLs, text content you upload
- **Usage Data**: API calls, feature usage, login patterns
- **Device Information**: IP address, browser type, device identifiers

## 2. How We Use Your Data

- **Service Delivery**: Process calls, manage AI training, generate reports
- **Improvement**: Analyze patterns to improve AI accuracy and platform stability
- **Compliance**: Verify identities, maintain audit logs, comply with regulations
- **Marketing**: Send product updates, security alerts, promotional emails (opt-in)
- **Legal**: Respond to lawful requests from authorities

## 3. Data Protection

- **Encryption**: TLS 1.2+ in transit, AES-256 at rest
- **Access Control**: Role-based access (RBAC) - admins only
- **Backups**: Redundant backups in multiple datacenters
- **Audit Logs**: All access logged for 90 days
- **DLP**: Automatic detection and blocking of sensitive data patterns

## 4. Data Retention

| Data Type | Starter | Growth | Enterprise |
|-----------|---------|--------|------------|
| Call Recordings | 7 days | 30 days | 90 days |
| Call Transcripts | 7 days | 30 days | 90 days |
| Leads & Tickets | Indefinite (until deletion) | Indefinite | Indefinite |
| KB Content | Indefinite | Indefinite | Indefinite |
| Audit Logs | 30 days | 60 days | 90 days |

## 5. Data Deletion & Portability

- **Request Deletion**: Anytime via dashboard Settings > Export Data
- **Processing Time**: 5-7 business days
- **Permanent Deletion**: Cryptographic deletion (unrecoverable)
- **Data Portability**: Export all data in CSV format within 5 days

## 6. Third-Party Sharing

We share data ONLY with:
- **Exotel**: Voice provider (see Exotel privacy policy)
- **Razorpay**: Payment processor (PCI-DSS certified)
- **AWS**: Cloud hosting (SOC 2 Type II certified)
- **Law Enforcement**: When legally required by court order

We DO NOT sell or share data with advertisers or brokers.

## 7. WhatsApp & SMS Compliance

- **Consent**: Explicit opt-in before sending messages
- **Opt-Out**: Users can reply "STOP" to unsubscribe
- **GDPR**: We comply with GDPR if customer is EU-based
- **TCPA**: We comply with US Telephone Consumer Protection Act
- **TRAI**: We comply with India's telecom regulations

## 8. Cookies & Tracking

- **Session Cookies**: Technical necessity (auto-delete after 24 hours)
- **Google Analytics**: Anonymized, aggregate analytics only
- **No Tracking Pixels**: We don't track you across websites

## 9. Security Measures

- **2FA**: Multi-factor authentication (TOTP, SMS OTP)
- **Penetration Testing**: Quarterly third-party pen tests
- **Vulnerability Scanning**: Weekly automated scans
- **Employee Training**: Annual security training (100% compliance)
- **Incident Response**: 2-hour response SLA for security breaches

## 10. International Data Transfer

- **Data Location**: India (AWS Mumbai region) by default
- **Multi-Region**: Enterprise can request EU/US hosting (additional fee)
- **Compliance**: Data subject to Indian law + applicable international regulations

## 11. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18. If discovered, data immediately deleted.

## 12. Privacy Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data anytime
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion (except legally required records)
- Port your data to another service
- Opt-out of non-essential communications

**Submit requests**: privacy@bolti.ai

## 13. Policy Changes

Changes effective 30 days after notification. Continued use = acceptance.
