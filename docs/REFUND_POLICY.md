# Refund Policy - Bolti AI

**Last Updated: November 28, 2025**

## Refund Eligibility

### Monthly Plans
- **No partial month refund**: If subscribed on Day 5, you're charged for full 30 days
- **Refund window**: None after first charge
- **Exception**: Billing error (double charge, wrong amount) → immediate refund

### Yearly Plans
- **5-day refund guarantee**: If NOT activated (zero calls made)
- **After 5 days**: Non-refundable
- **Exception**: System failure >7 consecutive days → 1-month credit

## Refund Process

1. **Initiate**: Dashboard > Billing > Request Refund
2. **Review**: Our approval team reviews within 48 hours
3. **Approval**: Refund request must meet policy criteria
4. **Processing**: 5-7 business days to your original payment method

## Non-Refundable Scenarios

- ❌ Partial month usage (after 5 days)
- ❌ Plan downgrade (retain higher plan until end of billing period)
- ❌ Cancellation before end date (no early termination refund)
- ❌ Failed verification (Exotel caller ID verification)
- ❌ Account suspended for ToS violations

## Automatic Refund (No Approval Needed)

✅ Double billing (automatic duplicate charge reversal)  
✅ Payment processed twice (system error)  
✅ Service unavailable >99.5% downtime in single month  
✅ Unauthorized charges (fraud verification completed)

## Upgrade/Downgrade

- **Upgrade**: Credit applied (prorated difference)
- **Downgrade**: Refund issued if lower plan cheaper (prorated)
- **Timing**: Changes effective on next billing date

## Cancellation

- **Effective**: End of current billing cycle
- **Notice Period**: 5 days before billing date
- **Data**: Access continues until period ends
- **Post-Cancellation**: All data deleted 30 days after cancellation

## Disputes

Unresolved? Escalate to:

**Support**: support@bolti.ai

**Response Time**: 48 hours

## Special Cases

### Free Trial (If Offered)
- 30-day free trial included in some promo codes
- Cancellation before Day 30 = no charge
- Auto-renews to plan price if not cancelled

### Annual Prepay
- Full year paid upfront = 8-10% discount applied
- Refund calculated as: (Days Remaining / 365) × Discount Price
- Minimum 5-day window applies
